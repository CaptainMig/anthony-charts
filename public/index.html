Methodology
Anthony Charts is an independent measurement platform that aggregates publicly verifiable scientific and institutional data into transparent composite indicators of global condition. Every input is sourced. Every weight is published. Every revision is logged.

This document is the editorial standards record for Anthony Charts. It defines what we measure, how we measure it, and the constraints we operate under. It is versioned and dated. Changes are never silent.

Published: February 24, 2026

Platform Statement
Anthony Charts measures directional change across publicly verifiable inputs. It does not forecast outcomes or prescribe action.

This platform is descriptive, not prescriptive. It does not tell you what to believe. It shows signal, inputs, and uncertainty ‚Äî and lets you conclude. When the data shows improvement where others expect decline, or decline where others expect optimism, the data publishes as measured. The numbers win over mood.

Neutrality here is not a tone choice. It is a structural constraint enforced by documented methodology, pre-declared weights, and public revision logs.
Measurement Philosophy
‚öñÔ∏è
Paired Opposites
Every destructive force has a countervailing force. Species extinction is paired with conservation coverage. Conflict deaths with peacekeeping reach. Wealth inequality with social mobility. This pairing is intentional ‚Äî it prevents the platform from being purely alarming, and it asks the more useful question: is the response proportional to the problem?
üî¨
Trusted Sources Only
Every meter draws from peer-reviewed data, established institutional databases, or primary government science agencies. NOAA, NASA, IUCN, ACLED, UCDP, World Bank, UN DPPA, Copernicus C3S, NSIDC, Breakthrough Listen. No aggregators. No media outlets. No social signals. The source is always published in the drawer of each meter.
üìÖ
Update Cadence
Each meter displays its update cadence ‚Äî daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annual. Values are refreshed when the underlying source publishes. Cadence is always shown on each card. We do not interpolate between updates or smooth values without disclosing it.
üßÆ
Arc Scale Logic
Each arc gauge fills from 0% to 100% based on a defined, meaningful scale ‚Äî not arbitrary. Temp Anomaly = position within the 0‚Äì2¬∞C Paris corridor. CO‚ÇÇ = position between 280ppm (pre-industrial) and 500ppm (extreme risk). Scales are pre-declared and do not change without a logged revision.
üìã
Composite Independence
World On Edge and World Goodness are independently constructed composites measuring different phenomena. They are not inverses of each other ‚Äî Goodness ‚â† 100 ‚àí Edge. Each draws from distinct input pools with separately justified weights. Their divergence or convergence is data, not design.
üìù
Revision Discipline
Weights are never silently adjusted. If a composite input changes, a weight shifts, or a source is replaced, it is logged in the Revision Record below with the date, the change, and the rationale. This platform is solo-authored ‚Äî the revision log is the editorial accountability layer.
